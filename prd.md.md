# PRD: Aapla Rajgurunagar Business Solutions

## 1. Project Overview
* **Product Name:** Aapla Rajgurunagar Business Media Kit
* **Objective:** A high-conversion website for a local media influencer to showcase reach, authority, and past client success to local and corporate advertisers.
* **Primary CTA:** "Grow Your Business - Call Now"

---

## 2. Upfront Impact Dashboard (The "Big Numbers")
The website's "Hero" section must feature a counter-style dashboard displaying:
1.  **Followers:** 110K+ (Community Authority)
2.  **Total Reach:** [X] Million (Monthly Brand Exposure)
3.  **Total Views:** [X] Million (Content Performance)
4.  **Clients Served:** 79 (Proven Business Experience)

### Suggested "Missing" Metrics (To impress big brands):
* **Local Density %:** Highlighting that **80%** of followers are from the **Rajgurunagar/Khed/Chakan** belt.
---

## 3. Functional Requirements

### A. Commercial Lead Generation
* **Direct Call Button:** A high-contrast "Call for Advertisement" button.
* **WhatsApp Direct Link:** Opens a chat with a pre-filled business inquiry message.
* **Inquiry Form:** Captures Business Name, Industry, and Interest (e.g., Reel, Story, or Event Coverage).

### B. Professional Portfolio
* **Client Marquee:** A scrolling horizontal bar showing  names of the **79 clients** served.
* **Recent Work (API):** Live grid of the latest 6-9 advertisement posts synced via Instagram Graph API.
* **ROI Testimonials:** Text-based quotes from business owners focusing on results (e.g., "Our sales doubled in one week").

---

## 4. Technical Specifications
- Both Mobile & Desktop friendly VERY MODERN ui with INSTAGRAM COLOUR THEME
---

## 5. Success Metrics
* **Lead Quality:** Conversion of visitors to phone calls/WhatsApp messages.
* **Pitch Efficiency:** Using the live dashboard during physical client meetings to close deals faster.
